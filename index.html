About the Clean AIT Mission

Cleanliness is not merely a practice but a vital component of a healthy and productive environment. In our collage cleanliness plays an essential role in fostering a conducive learning atmosphere. Maintaining cleanliness not only promotes physical health but also enhances the overall educational experience for students, teachers, and staff.

Top Cleanliness Tips

Ensure grounds and public places are kept clean by properly disposing of waste.

Plant trees to promote a clean and green environment.

Join or organize community cleaning drives to keep your neighborhood tidy.

Segregate waste into biodegradable and non-biodegradable for better waste management.

Maintain personal hygiene by washing hands regularly and using clean water.
